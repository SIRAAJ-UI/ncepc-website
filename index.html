About us

National Competative Examination
Preparation Center (NCEPC)

With the exponential increase of competition, every student is looking to get the best opportunities to get ahead of other students. Competitive exams like NEET and JEE are great milestones for majority of the students to succeed in life.

Understanding this, NCEPC thrives to provide all that a student may need to excel. We guarantee qualified faculty members, streamlined courses, and the best learning environment for each of our students so that they never lack behind. We at NCEPC promise to provide the complete coaching management that you desire. We work with industry experts to work on continuous improvement of our services. We regularly work to improve the coaching strategies and content advancements to help prepare our students to compete with style and ease. Our flexible educational calendar and detailed unit lectures help every student to be fully prepared for competitive examinations.
