About us
About the Institute

NCEPC provides a blend of virtual and classroom learning and self-study in the form of recorded video lectures. Courses at NCEPC prepares students for their highly important NEET& JEE exams. Detailed lectures on each concept delivered by experienced faculty are a major part of the study plan at NCEPC. Other than this, by use of images, animation, practical examples, numerical and effective examples are modernized ways used at NCEPC to help students better understand the concepts. Students can study and revise each lecture by repeating it as many times as they want.

About the Institute

To help students prepare well for National Level Medical & Engineering entrance exams, experienced NCEPC faculty has carefully curated the complete study material. With recorded video lectures, e-Books, and assessments, NCEPC helps students achieve their true potential.

We Design and deliver NEET & JEE programs across a variety of disciplines. Considering the importance of NEET & JEE exams we have worked upon the knowledge-base with the help of our highly qualified faculties, our research, and our training experience, to offer a rich learning experience to our students.

Our Team

Our team comprises highly qualified teachers with 20 plus years of professional university teaching experience. They know the mentality of the students and know how to deliver technical concepts. They do not use traditional teaching methods instead they read the minds of the students and use the strategies by which students can grasp and maintain knowledge for a long time.
